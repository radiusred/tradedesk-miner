    - `#[inline] pub(super) fn one_way_anova(groups: &[Vec<f64>]) -> AnovaResult` — classic textbook:
      1. Compute group means and grand mean
      2. SS_between = Σ_i n_i * (μ_i - μ_grand)²
      3. SS_within = Σ_i Σ_j (x_ij - μ_i)²
      4. MS_between = SS_between / (k-1); MS_within = SS_within / (N-k)
      5. F = MS_between / MS_within
      6. p_value = 1 - FisherSnedecor(k-1, N-k).cdf(F)

Pool all values across groups; rank using average-rank tie correction (reuse rank_with_ties from cross/corr_rolling/kernel.rs OR replicate locally — Plan picks: replicate locally to avoid cross-family dependency, OR widen visibility on cross::corr_rolling::kernel::rank_with_ties to pub(crate).       4. p_value = 1 - ChiSquared(k-1).cdf(H_corrected)
