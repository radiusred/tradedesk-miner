<objective>
Implement three single-shot Single-arity bucketed SEAS scans following Pattern A + Pattern D (bucketed vector-output): SEAS-01 hour-of-day (24 buckets, UTC hour), SEAS-02 day-of-week (7 buckets, 0=Mon..6=Sun), SEAS-03 trading-session (4 buckets: Asia/London/NY/overlap, configurable UTC boundaries with FX-major defaults from RESEARCH.md §1.8).

Per RESEARCH.md §1.3: bucketed scans set effect.value = max-abs t-stat across buckets (the strongest bucket signal); per-bucket arrays land in effect.extra.{buckets, means, stds, counts, t_stats}.

Purpose: bucketed seasonality scans are the simplest SEAS family — pure aggregation by a temporal key over the returns vector. They share a kernel-helper pattern (`bucket_stats(returns, bucket_keys, num_buckets) -> BucketResult`) but differ in how they derive the bucket key. After this plan, half of SEAS is shipped (3 of 6).
Output: 3 new registered scans + 3 integration tests + a shared bucket-stats helper in seas/mod.rs OR a primitives/bucketing.rs module (Plan decides).
</objective>
